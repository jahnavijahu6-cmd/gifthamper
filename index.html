About DEE Hamper Studio

Founded in Hyderabad, DEE Hamper Studio specialises in creating beautifully curated gift hampers for life's most precious moments — from bridal showers and baby welcomes to engagements and celebrations of all kinds.

Every hamper is thoughtfully designed with premium products, artisanal goods, and heartfelt touches that make recipients feel truly special. We pour love and care into every basket, box, and bow.

Our team of gifting experts personally selects each item, ensuring quality, beauty, and meaning in every single hamper we create.
